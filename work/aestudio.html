Design Assignments for AE Studio

Can a startup guy have the agency eye?

June 11th, 2021

I was mildly surprised when Ben reached out to me about joining AE Studio, because I usually only hear from startups and not agencies. Then I was really surprised about how much I liked everything I found during my research. I haven't considered joining an agency since the early years of my career and as a result, didn't realize how much the game has changed in the interim.

The more I read, the more my curiosity was piqued and the questions mounted. A workplace that has the boredom-killing excitement of external clients, the entrepreneurial innovation of an internal skunkworks initiative, and a humane ethos driving it all? Is this combo really possible?

For the first time in a decade, I'm seriously considering working at a new type of organization... so let's dive into the design exercise and see how we mesh.
